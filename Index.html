General Information

Japan is an island nation in east asia located on the pacific ocean with
neighboring countries such as China , North and South Korea aswell as Russia.
As of today Japan has an estimated amount of 124,352,000 people living in japan.
Japan is used to having earthquakes and tsunamis due to japan being located in
the pacific ring of fire.

Interesting Facts

Japan's official national sport is sumo wrestling and it is also customary to
remove shoes when entering a home, inn or temple. Mount Fuji is the country's
most tallest peak and is also an active stratovolcano as well. Japan is also very well known
for their very fast and quick bullet trains that run throughout the country of Japan.
