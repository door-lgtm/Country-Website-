Activities in Japan

Karaoke

The first karaoke machine was invented in Japan in 1971 by a Japanese musician and inventor.
There is a karaoke world championships that began in 2003 in findland.
Billie jean by Micheal Jackson is the worlds most popular and most played karaoke song.

Hot Springs

In Japan hot springs are called "onsen" and represent a big part of Japanese culture.
There are thousands of natural hot springs all over Japan and are often located in or near volcanic regions.
People go to hot springs and bathe in the water because it is belived that it has therapeutic benefits.

Tours

Tokyo,kyoto,Osaka,Hiroshima and Mount fuji are some of the most popular places to visit and tour in Japan.
The best way to get around and tour Japan is through using the Japanese trains.
In the 2024 Travel and Tourism Competitiveness Report that Japan ranked 3rd out of 141 countries.
