Japanese Food

Sushi

Sushi normally consists of medium grain white rice and seafood inside of it.
Although there are many varieties of sushi there are seven main types.
Hako,Chirashi,Temaki,Uramaki,Maki,Shashimi and Nigiri are the seven main types.

Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup that comes with a variety of vegetables and Rich flavoured broth.
Raoraoken became the first true ramen shop that opened in Asakusa,Tokyo in 1910.
Ramen could also come in diffrent flavor soup bases as well as instant ramen coming in a bunch of flavors.

Sashimi

Sashimi is a Japanese dish that is made up of thinly sliced raw fish or seafood served with soy sauce,wasabi and pickled ginger.
The most popular types of fish used for sashimi are Tuna,Yellowtail,Mackerel,Sea bream and Flounder.
Sashimi is very similar to sushi with both using raw seafood except sashimi does not use rice unlike sushi.
