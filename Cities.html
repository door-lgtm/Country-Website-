Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and home to the shibuya crossing.

Hiroshima

Hiroshima is a modern city on Japan's honshu island.
Hiroshima was largely destroyed by an atomic bomb during world war 2.
In the present day there are 1.201 million people living in hiroshima.
